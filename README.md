# Skepsiz NWO Cybermobbing Musiker GRU Influence Research

This project conducts comprehensive research on the German musician Skepsiz, exploring various aspects including potential connections to New World Order (NWO) narratives, cybermobbing incidents, GRU (Russian military intelligence) influence operations, and broader cultural/political influences on the artist and their work.

## Research Focus

- **Artist**: Skepsiz
- **Spotify Profile**: [https://open.spotify.com/artist/2pXVzWvuotVDV5KpoCKRP5](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2pXVzWvuotVDV5KpoCKRP5)
- **Key Areas**:
  - Musical career and discography analysis
  - Online presence and social media influence
  - Potential cybermobbing incidents
  - GRU-related influence operations
  - NWO conspiracy theory connections

## Methodology

The research utilizes AI-powered agents and web scraping tools to gather data from public sources including:

- Spotify artist profiles
- Social media platforms
- News articles and forums
- Music streaming analytics

## Tools and Technologies

- **MCP Tools**: browsermcp for automated web browsing and data extraction
- **AI Agents**: Agentic AI assistants for data analysis and research coordination
